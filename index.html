Our Story

Trusted By 200 + satisfied clients

Lusid Catering, established over 7 years ago, specializes in catering, hospitality, and consultancy. Our expertise spans Chandelling, fast-food consulting, and managing camps, vessels, rigs, and houseboats for the Oil & Gas and manufacturing sectors. With in-house professionals, we deliver globally, tailoring menus within budget and maintaining high hygiene standards. Operating across three diverse fields, we guarantee five-star hospitality, serving an average of 96 persons daily. Partner with us for satisfied personnel and enhanced productivity.
